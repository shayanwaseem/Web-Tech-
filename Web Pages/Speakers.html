Keynote Speakers at WIEN 2017

Pastor Annie West, GA.

Rev. West birthed the Women of destiny annual women’s conference at Newark Gospel Tabernacle and Trinidad, W.I. While attending Newark Gospel Tabernacle, she served as an Elder, Christian Education Superintendent and leader of the Counselor/Home Visitation Team. She published her first book entitled “A Prisoner of Jesus Christ” in 2000. Rev. West was affirmed an apostle in April 2011. Rev. West is a devoted mother and grandmother

Min. Marcia Walker

Marcia has been blessed to be a Supervising Therapist and Independent Social Worker. She has experienced the beauty of God’s love and has been transformed from brokenness to a woman that God uses to bring deliverance to women every day. Today she ministers through her private Christian counseling practice at Wellspring Counseling, women’s conferences, facilitates professional Mental Health and other trainings around Columbus and the country of her birth, Guyana, South America.

Sis Swetha Sagar

She was called to ministry six years ago when she started with street evangelism, teaching and discipling people to follow Christ. She currently leads the young adult ministry: Revelation Generation in her church and teaches an online webinar. She teaches on discipleship, evangelism and applying Gods principles to our everyday life.
